Vision:

Our vision is to cultivate a transformative commerce program that integrates interdisciplinary learning, fosters critical thinking, and prepares students for dynamic careers in the global business arena.


Overview:

Explore our comprehensive commerce program designed to provide a well-rounded education in finance, accounting, and business management.

Our HEBA track seamlessly integrates history, economics, business studies, and accountancy, offering a unique blend of humanities and commerce. Alternatively, delve into CEBA to delve into computer science, economics, business studies, and accountancy, preparing for success in today's dynamic business landscape.

These courses equip students with the skills and knowledge for diverse careers, laying a solid foundation for professional growth and success.
