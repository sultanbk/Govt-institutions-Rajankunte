Vision:

"Our arts stream envisions fostering visionary artists and critical thinkers who embrace creativity as a catalyst for cultural understanding and positive change.

We aspire to cultivate a community where students emerge as empathetic individuals, equipped to navigate diverse perspectives and contribute meaningfully to a dynamic global landscape."


Overview:

Step into the immersive world of our holistic arts program at the Pre-University College (PUC) section. Discover a vibrant blend of literature, visual arts, performing arts, and cultural studies that ignite creativity and foster critical thinking.

Our curriculum, including courses in HEPS (History, Economics, Political Science, and Sociology), offers diverse avenues for exploring human expression and societal dynamics.

Join us to unleash your creativity, explore cultural narratives, and prepare for a future enriched by diverse artistic expressions and intellectual depth."
